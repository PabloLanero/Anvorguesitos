OUR HISTORY

Hambearguesería was born in Zaragoza in 2025, when a group of friends who loved good food and Aragon decided to combine their two passions: burgers and local products.

It all started in a small kitchen, testing recipes until we found the perfect combination. We wanted to create something different, burgers where every ingredient told a story of our land.

From a modest place, we became known for capturing the essence of Aragon in every detail, from the names of our burgers to the decoration.

Today we maintain that same spirit: same recipes, same suppliers and the same love for sharing the best of our land.
