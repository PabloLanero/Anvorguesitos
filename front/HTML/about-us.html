OUR HISTORY

Hambearguesería was founded in Zaragoza in 2025 by a group of friends passionate about good food and Aragón, who decided to combine their two loves: burgers and local ingredients.

It all began in a small kitchen, experimenting with recipes until we discovered the perfect combination.

Starting from a modest spot, we became known for capturing the essence of Aragón in every detail, from the names of our burgers to the decor.

Today we maintain that same spirit: same recipes, same suppliers and the same love for sharing the best of our land.
